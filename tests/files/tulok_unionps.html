Agendas and Minutes

Union Public Schools will make available the date, time, place and agenda of each regularly scheduled school board meeting and special or emergency meetings, in compliance with HB 1276 of the Oklahoma legislature.

All regular meetings of the Union Public Schools Board of Education are conducted on the second Monday of each month (*unless otherwise noted), beginning at 7 p.m., in the Education Service Center Board Room, 8506 East 61st Street, Tulsa, Oklahoma. For additional information, call 918-357-6015.
