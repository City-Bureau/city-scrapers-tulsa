The BOCC meets every Monday at 9:30 a.m., in Room 132 of the Tulsa County Headquarters Building, 218 W. 6th St., Tulsa, Oklahoma. If Monday is a holiday, the meeting will be the following Tuesday at 8:30 a.m.. The Commissioners may also hold special meetings as required.
